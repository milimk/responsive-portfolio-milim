Design Work

BrainTest is a cross-platform application available on iOS, Android, Amazon, and the web. It's an at-home screening test designed to detect early signs of cognitive impairment, such as Alzheimer's and dementia.

I was responsible for conceptualizing and creating the best user experience design for both consumer and business products, from crafting flowcharts and wireframes to developing prototypes and delivering the final product.

The products are tailored for senior individuals, which require different design approaches, including larger fonts, simplified navigation, and clear visual cues to ensure ease of use and accessibility.

Mini-Me is an e-learning and entertainment brand for children that includes multiple products, such as videos, games, books, an academy, and more.

From crafting the right user experience design to delivering the final product with content management support and maintaining the live product by analyzing key metrics, this project provided me with a full range of product development experience.

Designing products for children requires special considerations, such as easy navigation and parental controls. It's essential to create engaging content that is both educational and entertaining to capture and maintain children's attention.
