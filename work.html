My Work

Design Work

BrainTest is a cross-platform application available on iOS, Android, Amazon, and the web. It's an at-home screening test designed to detect early signs of cognitive impairment, such as Alzheimer's and dementia.

I was responsible for conceptualizing and creating the best user experience design for both consumer and business products, from crafting flowcharts and wireframes to developing prototypes and delivering the final product.

The products are tailored for senior individuals, which require different design approaches, including larger fonts, simplified navigation, and clear visual cues to ensure ease of use and accessibility.

BrainTest Website

Mini-Me is an e-learning and entertainment brand for children that includes multiple products, such as videos, games, books, an academy, and more.

From crafting the right user experience design to delivering the final product with content management support and maintaining the live product by analyzing key metrics, this project provided me with a full range of product development experience.

Designing products for children requires special considerations, such as easy navigation and parental controls. It's essential to create engaging content that is both educational and entertaining to capture and maintain children's attention.

Mini-Me Website

At AirG Inc., I have managed over five projects, overseeing everything from development to maintenance, including full user support and content management. In addition, I have helped enhance internal tools to improve the product development process.

To streamline the product development process, I worked on improving internal tools to enhance the user experience for the team. These improvements included content management and Customer Care User Management tools.

Jelly Splash is a match-3 puzzle game featuring colorful, fun jellies to pop. It is supported on multiple platforms, including iOS and Android phones and tablets. Developed and published by Wooga, the game was released in 2013.

I was responsible for creating all the user interface elements in the game, including the HUD, menus, icons, buttons, seasonal graphics, and more. I also assisted with designing marketing visuals to enhance the game's online presence, such as on app stores, KakaoTalk messenger, online blogs, and other platforms.

Jelly Splash Website

Company of Heroes 2 is a real-time strategy game for PC, set during the Eastern Front battles of World War II, where players control the Soviet Red Army. Developed by Relic Entertainment and published by SEGA, the game was released in 2013.

I was involved in creating the user interface for two expansion packs, Western Front Armies and Ardennes Assault, both featuring rich art styles that support WWII themes. The UI elements were designed with inspiration from gameplay elements such as metal, guns, dirt, and rough textures to enhance the game's immersion and engagement.
