About Me

Hi, my name is Milim Kim-Sinoy.

I am an experienced designer with over 10 years of professional career, during which I have trained my skills in creating visually compelling and user-centered designs across various platforms. My journey in the design world has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of aesthetics, user experience, and the importance of seamless interaction between users and digital products.

With a strong foundation in design principles and a passion for creating intuitive experience, I decided to become a Front-End Developer. This transition was driven by my desire to bring my designs to life through code, ensuring that the visual elements I create are not only beautiful but also functional and responsive.

My expertise now spans the entire process of product development, from initial design concepts to the final implementation of front-end code. I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and various frameworks and libraries, which I leverage to build clean, efficient, and scalable code. My background in design gives me a unique perspective as a developer, allowing me to bridge the gap between design and development teams effectively.

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest industry trends, tools, and technologies. My goal is to create seamless, user-friendly product experiences that not only meet business objectives but also delight users.
